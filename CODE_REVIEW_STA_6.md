### Цветовая схема

#### Light Theme
- Background: `bg-gray-50`
- Cards: `bg-white`
- Text: `text-gray-800`
- Borders: `border-gray-300`

#### Dark Theme
- Background: `dark:bg-gray-900`
- Cards: `dark:bg-gray-800`
- Text: `dark:text-gray-100`
- Borders: `dark:border-gray-600`
